Dead Pixels™ was formed on the 10/8/2024 with the express purpose of giving gamers
unique experiences that focused on societal issues that most bigger studios
wouldnt tackle. Each game is lovingly crafted by our indie dev team designed to
make you both feel enjoyment and facinated. We hope our games can inspire the
hearts of gamers to do more outside just playing games. Secondly a 35% of sales
revenue will be donated to charity with the rest being re-invested into the
company to pay employees and hopefully make better and better games.
