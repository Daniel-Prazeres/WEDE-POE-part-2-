Between Flesh and steel

Between Flesh and steel is a squad-turn-based game set in the distant future
where 87% of labor has been replaced by machines where you play as a recently
retrenched office worker, Steve. After Steve recieves the news he decides to take
up with the CEO of ultra corp. Steve must fight against hoards of robots in order
to give the big man upstairs a piece of his mind. Recruit other members of the
office to aid you in your quest to sucker punch the CEO.
