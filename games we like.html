Neo dystopia

A game by Red Rabbit productions

Neo dystopia is a topdown turn-based tactial shooter. You play as R-274 an
experimental super soldier who has recently escaped a goverment facility that has
been experimenting on people without their consent. You must dodge and weave
through sterile labs, barracks and break rooms to finally escape the facility
